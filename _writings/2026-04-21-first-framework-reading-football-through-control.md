## Why control enters the conversation

Control theory does not need to replace football language. It only needs to show that football can also be read as a system of state, adjustment, timing, and interaction.

## What this framework is for

The goal is not to flatten the game into abstraction, but to create a disciplined vocabulary for understanding movement, choice, order, and response.
